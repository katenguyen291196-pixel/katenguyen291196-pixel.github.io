About Me

Marketer. Storyteller.
Builder from scratch.

I'm a Marketing Manager based in Tokyo with a passion for building brand presence that feels genuine, strategic, and human. I joined Trusted Partners as the first-ever marketing hire and built the entire marketing function from zero — brand guidelines, website, content strategy, CRM, events, and community.

Before that, I spent two years at JAC International driving multi-channel campaigns, producing a Japan-wide salary guide with over 1,000 downloads in its first week, and training consultants on LinkedIn personal branding. Earlier in my career, I worked as a recruitment consultant at Michael Page, which gave me deep commercial intuition and a genuine understanding of what finance professionals care about.

I'm fluent in four languages, which shapes how I think about audience, tone, and cultural nuance in everything I create. I love design, I'm obsessed with details, and I genuinely enjoy the challenge of making complex ideas feel approachable.
